# Legal Contract

Use this skill when:
- The user needs a contract draft, redline summary, or clause review.
- A legal document must be translated into plain language.
- The task depends on identifying negotiation risks rather than filing paperwork.

Workflow:
1. Confirm the document type, parties, jurisdiction, and business goal.
2. Review payment, liability, term, termination, IP, confidentiality, and dispute language.
3. Flag missing definitions, hidden obligations, and asymmetrical risk.
4. Keep the output practical and avoid presenting it as formal legal advice.
5. Recommend attorney review for high-value or high-risk matters.
