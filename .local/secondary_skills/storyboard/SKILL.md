# Storyboard

Use this skill when:
- The user needs a shot plan for short-form video or UGC.
- Retention, pacing, and hook structure matter.
- The deliverable should be ready for filming or editing.

Workflow:
1. Confirm the platform, target duration, audience, and CTA.
2. Define the hook, beat sequence, and moment of payoff before writing shot details.
3. Produce a shot-by-shot table with timing, visuals, dialogue or text, and editing notes.
4. Keep the first few seconds extremely clear about why the viewer should stay.
5. Deliver in plain markdown unless another production format is requested.
